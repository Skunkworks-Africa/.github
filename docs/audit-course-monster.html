Internal Audit Report for Course Monster

Conducted thorough internal audits for Course Monster, resulting in seamless external certification and identification of key improvement areas.

Audit Overview

The internal audits conducted for Course Monster covered the following key areas:

  • Compliance with ISO9001 standards
  • Effectiveness of the Quality Management System (QMS)
  • Process efficiency and consistency
  • Employee training and competency
  • Customer feedback and satisfaction

Detailed Findings

During the audit, several findings were identified, along with recommendations for improvement:

  • Document Control:
    • Finding: Several documents were outdated and not properly version-controlled.
    • Recommendation: Implement a document control system to ensure all documents are up-to-date, properly version-controlled, and easily accessible to relevant staff.
    • Action Taken: A new document control system was implemented using a centralized digital platform, ensuring all documents are current and accessible.
